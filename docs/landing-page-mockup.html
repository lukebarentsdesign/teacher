What TeachBase does today

Everything a teaching business needs to stay organised

The essentials for running a self-employed teaching or coaching practice — clients, payers, locations and money — ready to use now.

Clients & payers in one place

Keep clients, their guardians and payment contacts together, with several clients linked to a single payer for family or group billing.

Locations & term calendars

Set up teaching locations, rooms and term calendars — schools, home visits, your studio or online.

Invoice & statement PDFs

Download a clear PDF statement or invoice for any client, built from their ledger entries.

Record payments

Record manual payments against a client's subscription and watch the running balance update automatically.

Make-up credits & cancellations

Accounting export

Flexible billing

The clever bit

Smooth monthly

A year of lessons spread into equal monthly payments.

Smooth monthly is the fairest plan — and the biggest headache to set up

Working out a year of lessons, dividing it evenly, handling term breaks and mid-year starts, then convincing every parent it's fair — most teachers give up and go back to lumpy termly invoices. TeachBase does the maths for you, spreads the year into equal payments, and hands you clear, transparent figures to share, so it's an easy “yes” for families. The guided smooth-payment calculator is in preview for the trial.
